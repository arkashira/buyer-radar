# Product Requirements Document (PRD) for buyer-radar

## Problem Statement
Early-stage startup founders often struggle to identify and prioritize high-value buyer segments for their AI products. Without actionable insights into buyer behavior and market trends, these founders risk misallocating resources, missing growth opportunities, and falling behind competitors. buyer-radar aims to solve this problem by providing a data-driven sales intelligence platform that helps founders make informed decisions to drive revenue growth.

## Target Users
- **Early-stage startup founders**: Particularly those in the AI product space.
- **Sales and marketing teams**: Within startups looking to optimize their sales pipeline and conversion rates.
- **Product managers**: Seeking to align product development with market demands and buyer segments.

## Goals
1. **Identify High-Value Buyer Segments**: Provide AI-powered analysis to pinpoint lucrative buyer segments.
2. **Optimize Sales Pipeline**: Offer data-driven recommendations to improve conversion rates and revenue growth.
3. **Market Trend Analysis**: Deliver real-time insights into emerging trends and opportunities.
4. **Competitive Landscape Analysis**: Offer comprehensive analysis of the competitive landscape to identify areas for differentiation.
5. **Actionable Insights**: Provide concrete, actionable recommendations to inform sales strategies and maximize ROI.

## Key Features (Prioritized)
1. **Buyer Segment Identification**
   - AI-powered analysis to identify high-value buyer segments.
   - Segmentation based on demographics, behavior, and purchasing patterns.
   - Visualization tools to present segment data clearly.

2. **Sales Pipeline Optimization**
   - Data-driven recommendations to optimize conversion rates.
   - Tools to track and analyze sales pipeline performance.
   - Integration with existing CRM systems.

3. **Market Trend Analysis**
   - Real-time insights into emerging trends and opportunities.
   - Alerts and notifications for significant market shifts.
   - Trend forecasting based on historical and current data.

4. **Competitive Landscape Analysis**
   - Comprehensive analysis of the competitive landscape.
   - Identification of areas for differentiation.
   - Tools to monitor competitor activities and strategies.

5. **Actionable Insights**
   - Concrete, actionable recommendations to inform sales strategies.
   - ROI analysis for different sales and marketing strategies.
   - Customizable dashboards to track key performance indicators (KPIs).

## Success Metrics
1. **User Adoption**
   - Number of active users and frequency of use.
   - User satisfaction scores and feedback.

2. **Sales Performance**
   - Increase in conversion rates and revenue growth.
   - Reduction in customer acquisition costs.

3. **Market Insight Utilization**
   - Number of insights generated and acted upon.
   - Impact of insights on strategic decision-making.

4. **Competitive Advantage**
   - Number of unique insights generated about the competitive landscape.
   - Impact on product differentiation and market positioning.

## Scope
### In-Scope
- Development of AI-powered analysis tools for buyer segment identification.
- Integration with existing CRM systems.
- Real-time market trend analysis and alerts.
- Comprehensive competitive landscape analysis.
- Customizable dashboards for actionable insights.

### Out-of-Scope
- Development of new CRM systems.
- Direct sales or marketing execution services.
- Hardware-related solutions.
- Non-AI-based market analysis tools.
